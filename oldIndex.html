Zed
Master Of Shadows

Introduction

Zed is one of the best assassins in league. His kit makes him mobile and able to close distances very easily while being able to burst enemies down with style. The fact that Zed can close large distances very quickly with Yomuus, W, and R while still having enough damage to burst down his target allows him to exert amount of pressure on the map, especially on ADCs. The one thing I love about Zed is how high his skill ceiling is as he has insane outplay potential with 3 means of repositioning himself in a fight (W, R, and Flash). While there are many counters to his ultimate, good Zed players will still be able to make an impact even in the late game.

Your typical runes against an AP mid. The 1 AD Glyph actually gives you 1 extra AD despite the bonus only being +0.28 AD due to rounding. Against a bursty AD laner (i.e Pantheon) you'd want to swap the health seals for armor seals. Also, if you're not worried about heavy AP burst damage you can replace all your glyphs for flat CDR glyphs.

Masteries

If you're running CDR glyphs, opt for this mastery tree. With CDR boots and Youmuus you will have 38% CDR. With near max CDR you can have 1 W shadow shoot 2 shurikens within its lifetime which can serve as great unexpected poke. If you're running MR glyphs instead then replace the points in the CDR mastery into the bonus AD mastery.
